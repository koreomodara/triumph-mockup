WELCOME TO ROCK SOLID

Following the Holy Spirit, we exist to create a movement of disciple-making disciples.

Rock Solid Church is committed to creating a movement of disciple-making disciples — men and women who reflect the diversity of our community and live in authentic unity. We believe church should feel welcoming, life-giving, and rooted in real connection.

GOSPEL

The gospel is what should characterize us. It should be our identity: We are a gospel people.

The gospel is the good news of Jesus: That Jesus lived a sinless life in my place, died the death that I deserved, and rose to life again. And whoever believes in Him has eternal life.
